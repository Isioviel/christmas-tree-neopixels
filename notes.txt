microUSB in - regulate power down to 3.3V for microbit
			- send full 5V to neopixels

			
microUSB -- switch + LED -- neopixels -- microbit
						 -- microbit  -- neopixels 	(pin0 + ground)
									  -- R pot		(pin1 + ground + 3V)
									  -- G pot		(pin2 + ground + 3V)
									  -- B pot		(pin8 + ground + 3V)
									  -- DIP		(pin12 + pin13 + pin14 + pin15 + ground)
									  -- T pot		(pin16 + ground + 3V)

RGB value taken from the three pots
speed taken from pot

0000	white solid
1000	white flashing
1100	white running
1110	white random
1111	white fading

0100	multi flashing
0110	multi running
0111	multi random

0001	multi choice solid
0010	multi choice flashing
0011	multi running
1011	multi choice random
1101	multi choice fading

white solid = rgb(60,60,60) and stay put
white flashing = random choice but all the same, keep changing
white running = rgb(60,60,60) one at a time then (0,0,0) repeat
white random = some on some off
white fading = start at (0,0,0) go up to (60,60,60) then back down again

multi flashing = random choice of rgb, keep changing
multi running = choose randomly, turn on one at a time, then repeat
multi random = some on, some off - choose randomly
